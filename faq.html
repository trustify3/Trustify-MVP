Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trustify?

+

Trustify is a decentralized investment platform that allows individual investors to pool their resources and participate in private seed rounds of high-potential crypto projects. It leverages the transparency and security of blockchain technology to provide easy access to exclusive investment opportunities.

How does Trustify work?

+

Trustify connects investors with carefully curated investment pools. After connecting your wallet to the platform, you can browse available pools, select the one that fits your investment preferences, and participate. Once your investment is made, you can track its performance and enjoy profits once the project succeeds.

Which cryptocurrencies can I use to invest?

+

Trustify primarily supports investments through Solana-based tokens, ensuring fast and low-cost transactions on the Solana blockchain. You can link your Solana wallet to the platform for seamless investment.

Is my investment secure on Trustify?

+

Yes, Trustify utilizes blockchain technology to ensure transparency, security, and accountability. All transactions are recorded on the Solana blockchain, and we only list projects that have undergone thorough vetting by experts.

How can I connect my wallet to Trustify?

+

To connect your wallet, simply click the "Connect" button on the Trustify platform, select your wallet provider, and follow the on-screen instructions. Trustify currently supports popular Solana wallets like Phantom and Sollet.

Can I withdraw my investment anytime?

+

Once you make an investment, it may be subject to the project’s lock-in period or conditions. However, Trustify ensures that your investments are easy to manage, and you can view all your holdings at any time.

How do I stay updated on new investment opportunities?

+

You can join our community on social media or subscribe to Trustify’s newsletter to stay updated on the latest investment pools and news. We also send notifications directly through the platform.

What are the fees for using Trustify?

+

Trustify charges a small fee for each successful investment to cover operational costs. The exact fee varies depending on the specific pool, and all fees are transparently displayed before you confirm your investment.

How do I join the Trustify community?

+

To join the Trustify community, simply click on the "Join Community" button on our homepage. You can follow us on social media, participate in discussions, and engage with other investors in the Trustify ecosystem.

How can I contact Trustify support?

+

For any inquiries, you can visit our "Contact" page, where you'll find our support email and social media links. Our team is available to assist you with any issues or questions you may have.
